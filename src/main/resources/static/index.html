About us

We are a group of people who support technology and love to build tools that make everyday life easier. Our mission is to help individuals and small teams manage their email contacts in a simple, secure, and intuitive way. No clutter, no complexity – just a clean space to stay connected.

Your Data is safe

Your privacy matters to us. All your data is encrypted and stored with the highest security standards. We don't share your information with third parties. What you put here, stays here – safe and protected.
